ArhiSna and ArhiTab

ARHI TAB and ARHI SNA are design firms that specialize in technologically functional projects. Our plans and designs are centered around heavy equipment and utility connections (e.g., electrical, water, gas). We are knowledgeable of workplace regulations and requirements, and our work is compliant with HACCP standards.We have designed and installed technological projects for over thirty years. We have completed projects for: catering companies hotels business offices medical and health centers transportation hubs (railway stations, airports, seaports) social services (student centers, day cares, schools, retirement homes, and disability centers) In addition to planning and design, we also supervise production and installation of heavy equipment. We work cooperatively with most Serbian architecture and construction firms, as well as many firms abroad (especially those in Russia and the Former Soviet Republics).
