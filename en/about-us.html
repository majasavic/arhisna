ArhiSna and ArhiTab

ARHITAB and ARHISNA is architecture studio specialized in technologically functional projects. Our work is centered around heavy equipment and utility connections such as electrical, water or gas. We constantly follow workplace regulations and requirements and our work is compliant with HACCP standards. Our company has expertise in technological projects within catering companies, restaurants for employees in factories, student centers, schools and kindergartens. In addition to this, our work covers hotels, administrative and business centers, medical and health centers, transportation hubs (railway stations, airports, dock buildings), social services (student centers, daycares, schools, retirement homes, and disability centers), sport centers, hypermarkets, shopping malls, various religious objects, etc. With over thirty years of experience in planning and designing, we assure you in our professionalizam with our extensive list of references. In addition to all of the above, we also supervise production and installation of heavy equipment. We work cooperatively with most Serbian architecture and construction companies, as well as many firms abroad. Most of our cooworkers that reside outside from Serbia are located in Russia and the Former Soviet Republics.
If you feel like we could work together, don't hesitate to contact us. We are looking forward to working with you!
